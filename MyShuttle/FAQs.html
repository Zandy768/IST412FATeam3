Frequently Asked Questions



** Question 1: What forms of payment can I use with MyShuttle service?
** Answer 1: MyShuttle App accepts Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, credit and debit cards, cash, and MasterCard or Visa branded commuter benefit cards.
** Question 2: How can I request a ride?
** Answer 2: You may request an app by going to our website or by downloading the application in your phone. You can choose your starting destination and select a nearby driver that is displayed in the map closest to you.
** Question 3: What is cancellation fee?
** Answer 3: We appreciate the partners’ time and, thus, we always compensate the time spent on the road to the passenger if the trip never took place.
** Question 4: Am I able to use MyShuttle application to ride with my pet?
** Answer 4: Yes, you may travel with your pets using our service.
